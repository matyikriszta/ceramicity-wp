21 Series Lighting by Omer Arbel for Bocci

By krisztinamatyi / May 23, 2014 / Companies, Designers, Studios / Leave a comment / Edit

21 Series Lighting by Omer Arbel for Bocci combines glass and porcelain by wrapping thin sheets of raw porcelain around frosted blown glass diffusers to create soft-light pendants designed for clusters. An analog fabrication process means that each pendant has a unique shape, of varying size. Omer Arbel heads his own Omer Arbel Office design practice in Vancouver, Canada.
